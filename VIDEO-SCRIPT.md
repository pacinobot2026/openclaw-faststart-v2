## PRODUCTION NOTES

**Visual Style:**
- Clean, minimal
- Black/white/yellow color scheme (matches page)
- Fast cuts for energy sections
- Slower, deliberate pacing for authority
- Text overlays for key points

**Music:**
- Subtle, modern
- Builds tension in problem section
- Confident in solution
- No generic motivational music

**Voice:**
- If AI voice: Confident, measured, not excited
- ElevenLabs: Use professional male voice (Adam or Josh)
- Pacing: Slightly slower than normal (authority)
- No hype inflection

**Screen captures to include:**
- Operator Version page scrolling
- Git commit timestamps
- Stripe dashboard (with numbers blurred if needed)
- Vercel deployment logs
- Twitter launch tweet

- Hook: 15 seconds (grab attention)
- Problem: 30 seconds (create tension)
- Difference: 45 seconds (position solution)
- Breakdown: 30 seconds (proof)
- Identity: 30 seconds (transformation)
- Offer: 30 seconds (clear value)
- Close: 15 seconds (decision point)
