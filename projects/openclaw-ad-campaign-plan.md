# 🚀 OpenClaw Ad Campaign Plan

**Status:** DRAFT - Ready for Review  
**Created:** 2026-02-23 2:30 AM PT  
**Goal:** Drive sign-ups, memberships, and awareness for OpenClaw platform

---

## 🎯 Campaign Overview

### Product: OpenClaw
- **What it is:** AI assistant platform that runs locally, controlled by users
- **Key differentiator:** Privacy-first, customizable, integrates with local tools
- **Target market:** Developers, entrepreneurs, small business owners, power users

### Campaign Objective
- **Primary:** Drive new user sign-ups and premium memberships
- **Secondary:** Build brand awareness in AI/automation space
- **Tertiary:** Grow community (Discord, social channels)

---

## 👥 Target Audiences

### Audience 1: Technical Entrepreneurs
- **Demographics:** 25-45, business owners, technically savvy
- **Pain points:** Too many tools, overwhelmed by manual tasks
- **Message angle:** "Your overnight employee - automates while you sleep"

### Audience 2: Developers/Tech Enthusiasts
- **Demographics:** 22-40, software developers, early adopters
- **Pain points:** Want control over AI, privacy concerns with cloud solutions
- **Message angle:** "Local-first AI you actually own and control"

### Audience 3: Small Business Owners
- **Demographics:** 30-55, solopreneurs, agency owners
- **Pain points:** Can't afford full-time staff, drowning in busy work
- **Message angle:** "AI assistant that handles your business 24/7"

---

## 📺 Ad Platforms Strategy

### Platform 1: Google Ads (Search)
**Budget:** $500-1000/month starting
**Ad Types:**
- Search ads targeting keywords like:
  - "AI assistant for business"
  - "local AI platform"
  - "private AI assistant"
  - "automation tools for entrepreneurs"

**Landing Page:** Dedicated campaign page highlighting key benefits + free trial CTA

### Platform 2: Facebook/Instagram Ads
**Budget:** $300-800/month starting
**Ad Types:**
- Video ads (30-60 second demos)
- Carousel ads (features showcase)
- Story ads (quick testimonials)

**Targeting:**
- Interests: Business automation, AI, entrepreneurship, software development
- Lookalike audiences from existing users

### Platform 3: LinkedIn Ads
**Budget:** $400-800/month starting
**Ad Types:**
- Sponsored content (thought leadership posts)
- Message ads (direct outreach to decision-makers)

**Targeting:**
- Job titles: Founder, CEO, CTO, Developer, Business Owner
- Industries: SaaS, Marketing, Consulting

### Platform 4: YouTube Ads
**Budget:** $300-600/month starting
**Ad Types:**
- Skippable in-stream ads (30-60 seconds)
- Discovery ads on relevant content

**Targeting:**
- Channels: Tech tutorials, business automation, productivity

## 📊 Success Metrics

### Primary KPIs
- **Sign-ups:** Target 50-100 new users per month (first 3 months)
- **Cost per acquisition (CPA):** Target $20-40 per sign-up
- **Conversion rate:** Landing page target 3-5%

### Secondary KPIs
- Click-through rate (CTR): Target 2-4%
- Engagement rate: Video view completion 30%+
- Quality score (Google Ads): 7+

### Tracking Setup
- Google Analytics with conversion tracking
- UTM parameters on all ad links
- A/B testing different ad copy/creatives
