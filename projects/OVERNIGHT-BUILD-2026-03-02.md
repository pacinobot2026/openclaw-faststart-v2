## Why Bridge Page (Not "Sales Page")?

MintBird API doesn't have a dedicated "sales page with checkout" endpoint. The available options are:
- **Bridge Pages** = Pre-sell pages that redirect to checkout
- **Lead Pages** = Opt-in pages

Bridge pages work PERFECTLY as sales pages - they're long-form, persuasive pages that redirect to Stripe/payment processor. Most people use them this way.
