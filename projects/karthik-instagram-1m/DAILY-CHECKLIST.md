# ✅ DAILY INSTAGRAM CHECKLIST
*Follow this EVERY day. No exceptions.*

---

## 🌅 MORNING (30 min)

- [ ] **Check AI news** (5 min)
  - Twitter: @OpenAI, @AnthropicAI, @Google
  - Reddit: r/artificial, r/ChatGPT
  - ProductHunt: New AI launches

- [ ] **Reply to all comments** from yesterday (10 min)
  - Thoughtful replies, not just emojis
  - Ask questions to keep conversation going
  - Pin your best comment

- [ ] **Engage on 10 other accounts** (15 min)
  - Leave genuine comments (not "🔥🔥🔥")
  - Be FIRST commenter when possible
  - Target accounts: 10K-100K followers (they reply)

---

## 🎬 MIDDAY (45 min)

- [ ] **Script today's reel** (15 min)
  - Follow the template from WEEK-X-SCRIPTS.md
  - Hook first, then value, then CTA

- [ ] **Record the reel** (20 min)
  - Film hook 3 times, pick best energy
  - Good lighting (face the window)
  - Speak with energy, not monotone

- [ ] **Edit the reel** (10 min)
  - Add captions (CapCut auto-caption)
  - Zoom cuts every 2-3 sec
  - Add text overlays for key points

---

## 📱 AFTERNOON (15 min)

- [ ] **Post at optimal time** 
  - Best times: 11am-1pm OR 7-9pm
  - Write a killer caption (hook + value + CTA)
  - Use 5-8 relevant hashtags

- [ ] **Post to Stories** (3 stories)
  - Story 1: "New post! Go watch 👆"
  - Story 2: Behind-the-scenes of recording
  - Story 3: Poll or question for engagement

---

## 🌙 EVENING (20 min)

- [ ] **Reply to all new comments** (10 min)
  - Algorithm boosts posts with active conversations
  - Reply within first 2 hours = more reach

- [ ] **Engage again** on 10 accounts (10 min)
  - Different accounts than morning
  - Be genuine, add value in comments

- [ ] **Prep tomorrow's content idea** (5 min)
  - Check calendar
  - Save any trending topics to use

---

## 📊 WEEKLY (Sunday)

- [ ] **Update TRACKER.md** with all stats
- [ ] **Review what worked vs what didn't**
- [ ] **Plan next week's content angles**
- [ ] **Study 5 viral reels in your niche**
- [ ] **DM 5 creators for potential collabs**
