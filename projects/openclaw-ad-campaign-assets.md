**Q: Is OpenClaw really private?**  
A: Yes. OpenClaw runs entirely on your machine. Your data never touches our servers, never goes to the cloud, never leaves your device. We literally cannot see your data - it's physically on your computer, not ours.

**Q: Do I need to be technical?**  
A: No. Installation takes 5 minutes (one command). The interface is designed for non-technical users. If you can use ChatGPT, you can use OpenClaw.

**Q: Can it really work while I'm asleep?**  
A: Yes. OpenClaw runs as a background service on your machine. You can close your laptop, go to bed, and it keeps working. Set tasks before bed, wake up to completed work.

**Q: What's the difference between Free and Pro?**  
A: Free includes basic automation and core features. Pro adds advanced workflows, priority support, unlimited skills, and team features. Most solo users start with Free and upgrade when they need more power.

**Q: How is this different from ChatGPT?**  
A: ChatGPT runs in the cloud (OpenAI reads everything you send). OpenClaw runs locally (your data stays on your machine). ChatGPT charges per message. OpenClaw has unlimited usage. ChatGPT can't access your local files. OpenClaw integrates with everything on your computer.

**Q: Will this slow down my computer?**  
A: No. OpenClaw is designed to run efficiently in the background. It uses minimal resources when idle and only activates when processing tasks.

**Q: Can I use it on multiple devices?**  
A: Yes. Install OpenClaw on as many devices as you want. Your settings sync across devices (if you enable sync).

**Q: What if I already use other AI tools?**  
A: Keep using them! OpenClaw works alongside other tools. Use ChatGPT for general questions, OpenClaw for sensitive business tasks.

**Q: Is there a refund policy?**  
A: Yes. If you're not satisfied within 30 days, email us and we'll refund your money. No questions asked.

**Q: How do updates work?**  
A: OpenClaw auto-updates in the background. New features and improvements install automatically. You never have to manually download updates.

**Q: Can I build custom automations?**  
A: Yes! OpenClaw has a skills system. You can write custom skills in JavaScript or install skills from the community. Full documentation available.

**Q: What about support?**  
A: Free users get community support (Discord). Pro users get priority email support (reply to any email from us). Response time for Pro users is typically under 4 hours.

**Q: Does this work on my operating system?**  
A: OpenClaw supports Windows, Mac, and Linux. If you can run Node.js, you can run OpenClaw.

**Q: How secure is it?**  
A: Very. Because everything runs locally, there's no cloud attack surface. Your data never leaves your machine, so there's nothing to breach. We use industry-standard encryption for any data that does sync (optional features only).
