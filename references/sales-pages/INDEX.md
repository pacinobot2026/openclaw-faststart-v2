## Design Patterns Observed

### Common Elements Across High-Performing Pages:
1. **Clear, benefit-driven headlines** (what you get, not what it is)
2. **Hero videos** with play buttons (11 out of 18 pages)
3. **Contrasting CTA buttons** (green, purple, yellow, orange)
4. **Social proof** (logos, testimonials, student counts)
5. **Benefit sections** (3-4 column grids with icons)
6. **Clean white or dark backgrounds** (high contrast)
7. **Countdown timers** (urgency on 3 pages)
8. **Before/After framing** in headlines
9. **Real human images** (not stock photos)
10. **Minimalist design** (lots of white space)

### Color Schemes:
- **High-converting CTAs:** Green, Purple, Yellow/Orange
- **Backgrounds:** White, Dark Navy/Black, Light Gray
- **Accents:** Yellow underlines, colored buttons, icons

### Typography Patterns:
- **Headlines:** Bold sans-serif OR elegant serif
- **Subheadlines:** Lighter weight, smaller size
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif, 16px+
- **Contrast:** Dark on light or light on dark (never medium on medium)
