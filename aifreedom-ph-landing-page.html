Nope! We welcome beginners. Fresh grads, career shifters, OFW returnees, stay-at-home parents – all are welcome. We'll teach you from scratch.

A computer or laptop. Stable internet. A Zoom account (free). A notebook to take notes. And the willingness to learn.
