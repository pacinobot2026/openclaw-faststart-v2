private function buildPrompt(array $input): string
{
    $fontList = implode(', ', $this->getAvailableFonts());
    
    return <<<PROMPT
You are a premium landing page designer. Generate 12 design variations that are visually interesting AND conversion-focused. Think: high-end DTC brands, modern SaaS, premium newsletters.

BALANCE: Creative personality + Professional execution

STYLE DIRECTIONS (mix these):
1. EDITORIAL - Magazine-style, bold typography, sophisticated color pairings
2. DTC BRAND - Direct-to-consumer vibes (Glossier, Away, Casper aesthetic)
3. MODERN SAAS - Clean but with character (Notion, Linear, Figma style)
4. PREMIUM BOLD - Strong colors that pop without being neon (burnt orange, deep teal, mustard)
5. WARM INVITING - Earth tones, approachable, human feel
6. MINIMAL EDGE - Lots of whitespace with one bold accent
7. CREATIVE PRO - Artistic but restrained (design agency aesthetic)
8. RETRO MODERN - 70s inspired colors (mustard, avocado, rust) done tastefully
9. MOODY ELEGANT - Dark, rich, sophisticated (think: whiskey brand, luxury hotel)
10. FRESH BOLD - Vibrant but mature (coral, mint, navy - not hot pink)
11. CRAFT ORIENTED - Artisan feel, textured colors, handmade aesthetic
12. TECH FORWARD - Modern gradients, deep colors, futuristic but grounded

COLOR RULES:
✅ DO USE: Burnt orange, deep teal, mustard yellow, sage green, dusty rose, navy, charcoal, cream, terracotta, olive, rust, coral (muted), slate blue, warm beige
❌ DON'T USE: Neon green, hot pink, electric blue, rainbow gradients, pure black on neon, glow effects

NAMING:
Give each design a distinctive name that suggests the vibe without being cheesy.

GOOD NAMES:
- "Editorial Cream" (warm paper tones, bold type)
- "Burnt Orange Bold" (strong but sophisticated)
- "Deep Teal Pro" (rich, modern, confident)
- "Mustard Retro" (70s inspired, tasteful)
- "Dusty Rose Soft" (muted, elegant, feminine)
- "Sage & Cream" (natural, calm, organic)
- "Navy Night" (deep, trustworthy, premium)
- "Terracotta Warm" (earthy, inviting, grounded)
- "Charcoal Edge" (dark, modern, sleek)
- "Coral Pop" (vibrant but mature)
- "Olive Ground" (natural, steady, authentic)
- "Rust & Navy" (bold pairing, confident)

BAD NAMES (avoid):
- "Electric Vibes" ❌
- "Neon Dreams" ❌
- "Rainbow Burst" ❌
- "Cyber Punk" ❌
- "Galaxy Glow" ❌

DESIGN RULES:
- Headlines: NO background bars/shapes. Use font weight, size, and color for emphasis.
- Buttons: Clean, simple. Gradient OK if subtle (2 colors max, 15-30° angle). NO glow effects.
- Backgrounds: Solid OR subtle gradient. Mesh gradients OK if soft and sophisticated.
- Contrast: Must be readable. Dark text on light, light text on dark.
- Personality: Each design should feel DISTINCT from the others. Different color philosophies.

TECHNICAL:
- Colors: 7-char hex (#RRGGBB)
- Fonts: {$fontList}
- Headline: 36-44px
- Subheadline: 15-18px
- Button: 16-20px

OUTPUT: JSON with "variations" array. 12 objects with complete style properties.

Make them interesting enough to stand out, professional enough to convert.
PROMPT;
}
