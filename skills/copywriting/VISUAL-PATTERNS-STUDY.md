# 📸 VISUAL PATTERNS FROM 18 SALES PAGE EXAMPLES

Studied 10 high-converting sales pages. Here's what they ALL do:

---

## 🎯 UNIVERSAL PATTERNS (Every Page Has This):

### 1. HERO SECTION FORMULA:
- **Large headline** (2-3x body text)
- **Person image OR video** (builds trust + engagement)
- **One clear CTA** (green or bright color)
- **Price visible** (on or near first screen)

### 2. CONTRAST SECTIONS:
- **Light → Dark → Light** pattern
- Never two similar backgrounds in a row
- Dark sections feel premium/serious
- Light sections feel open/accessible

### 3. LARGE NUMBERS:
- **Price** is HUGE (4-6x body text)
- **Stats** are prominent (120,000 students, 760 MILLION opportunities)
- **Timers** when present are BIG
- Numbers = certainty

### 4. VIDEO PLACEMENT:
- 7 out of 10 pages have video in hero
- Always with play button overlay
- Either full-width OR side-by-side with copy

### 5. ICON BOXES:
- 3-column grid for benefits
- Icons above text
- White/light background boxes on dark sections
- Clean, scannable

---

## 🎨 COLOR PSYCHOLOGY PATTERNS:

### CTAs (Most Saturated Colors):
- **Green** (5 pages) - "Go, safe, money"
- **Orange/Red** (3 pages) - "Urgency, action"
- **Blue** (2 pages) - "Trust, calm"

### Backgrounds:
- **White/Light gray** - Premium, clean, open
- **Dark navy/black** - Authority, serious, premium
- **Bright accent** (blue, gold) - Energy, excitement

### Text:
- **Black on white** - Main body
- **White on dark** - Hero/feature sections
- **Accent color** - Numbers, highlights, CTAs

---

## 📐 LAYOUT PATTERNS:

### Hero Layouts (3 types seen):

**Type A: Full-Width Video** (RainMaker, CopyHackers)
- Video dominates
- Headline above
- CTA below
- Minimal distraction

**Type B: Split (50/50)** (EasyWebinar, Digital Marketer)
- Copy on left
- Image/video on right
- Price box included
- More information density

**Type C: Minimal + Image** (Full Focus, Dr. Peterson)
- Premium feel
- Large lifestyle/person image
- Minimal text
- Lots of white space

### Section Rhythm (ALL pages follow this):
1. Hero (light or dark)
2. Benefits grid (opposite of hero)
3. Problem/agitation (often dark)
4. Solution details (light)
5. Social proof (varies)
6. Offer (usually light with price)
7. Guarantee (often boxed)
8. Final CTA

---

## 🔥 COPYWRITING PATTERNS:

### Headlines Use These Formulas:

**Specific Number + Benefit:**
- "Get Back 80% Of Your Leads In Less Than An Hour"
- "200-300% MORE MUSCLE"

**Transformation:**
- "From Burnout to Balance"
- "Become A Certified E-Commerce Marketing Master"

**FOMO/Opportunity:**
- "You're Missing Out On 760 MILLION Opportunities"
- "Exclusive Offer"

### Subheadlines Add:
- **Timeframe** ("In Less Than An Hour", "Two 30-Minute Sessions")
- **Mechanism** ("With AI", "Using This Proven Method")
- **Authority** ("Taught by Dr. Jordan Peterson")

---

## 🚀 URGENCY TACTICS (What Works):

### Countdown Timers:
- **Top banner** (most aggressive)
- **Near CTA** (moderate pressure)
- Colors: Red/orange for urgency

### Scarcity Language:
- "Class starts Monday at 11:00 AM PDT"
- "Just $295" (implies regular price higher)
- "Exclusive Offer" (limited availability)

### Social Proof Numbers:
- "126,000+ Companies Like..."
- "120,000 students have joined"
- "4.5 out of 5 rating"

---

## 💰 PRICING DISPLAY PATTERNS:

### What Works:

**Large Price:**
- 4-6x body text
- Bold weight
- Accent color

**Context Around Price:**
- Strikethrough "regular" price above
- "Save 76%!" in accent color
- Payment options (Annual/Monthly)
- "Total Value: $1,209 Save 76%!"

**Price Boxes:**
- Background color different from page
- Border or shadow
- Price + CTA in same box
- "Billed annually @ $862" (transparency)

---

## 🎁 BENEFIT SECTION PATTERNS:

### "Here's What You'll Get" Structure:

**3-Column Icon Grid:**
- Icon/emoji at top
- Bold headline
- 2-3 lines of description
- White boxes on dark OR outlined boxes on light

**Common Benefits Listed:**
- Support/community
- Speed ("Set Up In 5 Minutes")
- Unlimited access
- Certification/authority
- Templates/resources

### Visual Pattern:
- Always uses icons (not photos)
- Consistent sizing
- Even spacing
- Scannable at a glance

---

## 📸 IMAGE PATTERNS:

### Person Images (7 out of 10 use):
- **Authority figure** (expert, teacher)
- **Smiling** (warm, approachable)
- **Professional attire** OR casual confidence
- **Eye contact** or looking at camera
- **Right side placement** (6 out of 7)

### Why This Works:
- Builds trust (real person)
- Social proof (they're successful)
- Aspirational (you could be them)

---

## ⚡ PSYCHOLOGICAL TRIGGERS USED:

### Authority:
- Credentials in headline ("Dr. Jordan Peterson", "Top Fitness Model")
- Logo walls (Shopify, Uber, HarperCollins)
- Student/customer counts
- Ratings (4.5 out of 5)

### Social Proof:
- "126,000+ Companies" 
- "120,000 students"
- Logo grids of trusted brands
- "Trusted by [big names]"

### Scarcity:
- Countdown timers
- "Class starts Monday"
- "Exclusive Offer"
- Limited seats/spots implied

### Urgency:
- Red countdown banners
- "Hurry! Class starts..."
- Time-limited bonuses
- "Save 76%!" (implies temporary)
