# Re-engagement Campaign - Progressive Hourly Pacing

## Campaign Structure

**Day 1: Warmup**
- 50 contacts sent at once (not hourly)
- Single batch to warm up reputation

**Day 2+: Hourly Pacing with Progressive Rates**
- Rate increases each day based on pace
- Hard cap: 400 contacts/hour
- Send hourly until daily total reached or contacts exhausted

---

## Hard Limit
- **Maximum:** 400 contacts/hour (never exceeded)

## Examples

### 10,000 contacts (Normal pace)
```
Day 1:  50 (warmup)
Day 2:  100/hr × 10hrs = 1,000
Day 3:  200/hr × 5hrs = 1,000
Day 4:  300/hr × 10hrs = 3,000
Day 5:  400/hr × 10hrs = 4,000
Day 6:  400/hr × 2.5hrs = 950 (completes)

Total: ~6 days
```

### 100,000 contacts (Aggressive pace)
```
Day 1:   50 (warmup)
Day 2:   200/hr × 10hrs = 2,000
Day 3+:  400/hr × 10hrs = 4,000/day (for ~25 days)

Total: ~26 days (1 day faster than Normal!)
```

---

## How It Works
1. **Day 1:** 50 contacts at once
2. **Day 2:** Start at base rate (50/100/100)
3. **Each day:** Increase rate (+50/+100/×2)
4. **At cap:** Stay at 400/hour until done
5. **Each hour:** Send one batch
6. **Per day:** Continue until all contacts sent or day ends
