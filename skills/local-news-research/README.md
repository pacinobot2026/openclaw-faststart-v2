# Local News Research Skill

Quickly find recent local news articles for newsletter content in any city or neighborhood.

## What It Does
- Uses Google News RSS to aggregate all local news sources automatically
- Filters by timeframe (1 hour, 24 hours, 7 days, 30 days)
- Cleans up irrelevant articles
- Categorizes stories by type (business, real estate, crime, community)
- Suggests newsletter angles and writing approaches

## How It Works
Primary: Google News RSS (`news.google.com/rss/search`) - aggregates all sources
Backup: Direct news sites (Review-Journal, local TV stations, etc.)

## When to Use
- "Find newsletter articles for [location]"
- "What's happening in [area] this week?"
- "Local news for my [city] newsletter"

## Example Output
Recent articles with dates, descriptions, and "why it matters" context for your readers.
