# Local News Sources by Region

## Las Vegas / Henderson / Summerlin
- **Las Vegas Review-Journal**: `https://www.reviewjournal.com/local/[area]/`
  - Summerlin: `/local/summerlin/`
  - Henderson: `/local/henderson/`
- **Las Vegas Sun**: `https://lasvegassun.com/news/[area]/`
- **8 News Now**: `https://www.8newsnow.com/tag/[area]/` (may block scraping)

## Finding Sources for New Cities
1. Search: `[city name] newspaper` or `[city name] local news`
2. Try patterns:
   - `[domain]/local/[neighborhood]/`
   - `[domain]/news/[area]/`
   - `[domain]/tag/[location]/`
3. Check TV station websites (usually have local news sections)

## Tips
- Major metro papers usually have neighborhood sections
- Local TV news sites often have searchable tags
- Some sites block automated access (403 errors) - try alternatives
