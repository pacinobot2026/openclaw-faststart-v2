---FAQ SECTION---

SECTION HEADLINE:
Questions? We Got You.

Q: Is this workshop really free?
A: Yes, 100% free. No credit card needed. After the workshop, there's an optional low-cost course if you want deeper training. But the workshop itself? Completely free.

Q: Will I get a job after this?
A: No guarantees. This workshop teaches you skills. Getting hired depends on your performance, client demand, and how well you apply what you learn. We're transparent about this.

Q: Do I need VA experience to join?
A: Nope! We welcome beginners. Fresh grads, career shifters, OFW returnees, stay-at-home parents – all are welcome. We'll teach you from scratch.

Q: What do I need to join the workshop?
A: A computer or laptop. Stable internet. A Zoom account (free). A notebook to take notes. And the willingness to learn.

Q: Will there be a replay if I miss a session?
A: Replays will be available for registered attendees only. But we highly recommend attending live – you'll get more value from the Q&A and interaction.

Q: What if I miss a day?
A: You can watch the replay, but each day builds on the previous one. Try to attend all three days for the full experience.

Q: How are top performers selected for the marketplace?
A: After the optional paid course, top performers go through skills assessments, interviews, and portfolio reviews. It's merit-based. Those who demonstrate skills, professionalism, and reliability get listed.
