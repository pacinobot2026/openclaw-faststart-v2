## PHASE 1: PRODUCT CLARITY (MANDATORY FIRST STEP)

Before writing ANY copy, extract:
1. What is the product? (Literal description)
2. What does buyer receive immediately after payment?
3. How is delivery fulfilled?
4. Who is the specific target buyer?
5. What painful problem does this solve?
6. What measurable outcome does it create?

**If unclear → ASK before writing**

Then summarize in ONE sentence:
**"This product is __ for __ so they can __."**

---

## PHASE 2: FRAMEWORK SELECTION

Pick ONE (don't mix):
- A) Problem → Agitate → Solve
- B) Before / After / Bridge
- C) Identity Shift
- D) Secret Mechanism
- E) Villain Narrative
- F) Speed Proof
- G) Case Study Narrative
- H) Opportunity Cost

---

## PHASE 3: PRIMARY EMOTIONAL DRIVER

Pick ONE (everything reinforces this):
- Speed
- Control
- Power
- Status
- Relief
- Leverage
- Money
- Simplicity

---

## PHASE 4: SALES PAGE STRUCTURE (FIXED ORDER)

1. **Headline** - Outcome + Mechanism (no vague hype)
2. **Opening Scenario** - Specific situation + frustration
3. **Problem Expansion** - Why current solutions fail
4. **Mechanism Section** - HOW this works (operational language, no magic)
5. **What You Get** - Bullet deliverables, clear post-purchase
6. **Price Justification** - Anchor value, explain why priced low
7. **Objection Handling** - Skill level, time, skepticism, support
8. **Risk Reversal** - Guarantee
9. **Clear CTA**

---

## PHASE 5: NO-MAGIC RULE (CRITICAL)

### ❌ FORBIDDEN LANGUAGE:
- "Built itself"
- "Autopilot"
- "While you sleep"
- "Push button riches"
- Vague AI hype

### ✅ USE OPERATIONAL LANGUAGE:
- "Generated"
- "Structured"
- "Connected via API"
- "Deployed"
- "Published"
- "Configured"

**Credibility > hype ALWAYS**

---

## PHASE 6: CLARITY AUDIT

After writing, answer:
- Can a 12-year-old explain what is being sold?
- Is delivery crystal clear?
- Is there any ambiguity about what happens after payment?

**If unclear → rewrite**

---

## PHASE 7: TENSION INJECTION CHECK

Page must contain:
- A specific scenario
- A specific frustration
- A specific cost of inaction
- A clear mechanism explanation

**If missing → revise**

---

## PHASE 8: SELF-SCORING

Score 1-10 on:
- Clarity
- Specificity
- Logical flow
- Offer transparency
- Emotional tension

**If any score < 8 → rewrite once**

---

## OUTPUT RULE

Only output final sales page.
Do NOT output:
- Internal reasoning
- Scores
- Process notes
