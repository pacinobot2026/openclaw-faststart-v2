## Lessons Learned

### Design Evolution
1. **Wireframe → Production:** Don't show wireframes as "done" - always add visual polish
2. **Modern SaaS aesthetic:** Gradients, glass morphism, shadows, animations are EXPECTED
3. **Uniform elements:** Cards same size = cleaner, more professional
4. **Icons > Text:** Compact, cleaner, more scannable

### Technical Wins
- `backdrop-blur-xl` + `bg-white/5` = perfect glass morphism
- Multi-color gradients (`from-blue-500 via-purple-500 to-pink-500`) = premium feel
- Animated shimmer overlays = perceived loading/activity
- `line-clamp-2` = consistent card heights without JS
- Hover scale + glow = engagement feedback

### Speed Matters
- Built emergency demo in 15 minutes (basic functionality)
- Iterated to premium design in 20 minutes (3 rounds of feedback)
- Total: 35 minutes from "need this NOW" to "wow" 🎬
