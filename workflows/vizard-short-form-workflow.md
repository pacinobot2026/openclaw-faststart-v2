# Vizard Short-Form Video Workflow

## Overview

**Vizard.ai** is an AI-powered video clipping tool that turns long-form videos into short viral clips for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and more.

**Perfect for:** Newsletter Hour recordings, trainings, masterminds, podcasts

---

## How Vizard Works

1. **Upload** your long-form video (up to 60 min on free, 600 min on paid)
2. **AI transcribes** and identifies engaging moments automatically
3. **One click** generates 30+ clips with captions and vertical reframing
4. **Edit/refine** using text-based editing (delete text = delete video)
5. **Export** or publish directly to social platforms

## Suggested Workflow for Chad

### Weekly Content Repurposing

**Source Videos:**
- Newsletter Hour (Monday, 2 hrs) → 20-40 clips
- Entourage Mastermind calls → 10-20 clips
- Training recordings → 15-30 clips

**Process:**
1. After call ends, download recording from Zoom/Vimeo
2. Upload to Vizard
3. Click "Get AI Clips"
4. Review clips (Vizard shows "virality score")
5. Export high-scoring clips
6. Schedule to social platforms

### Optimal Clip Settings

| Platform | Ratio | Duration | Notes |
|----------|-------|----------|-------|
| TikTok | 9:16 | 30-60 sec | Hook in first 3 sec |
| Instagram Reels | 9:16 | 15-30 sec | Fast cuts work best |
| YouTube Shorts | 9:16 | 30-60 sec | Educational content wins |
| LinkedIn | 1:1 or 9:16 | 30-90 sec | Professional tone |

## Quick Start Command

When ready to process a video:

```
/vizard [vimeo-url]
```

**This would:**
1. Download video from Vimeo
2. Upload to Vizard via API
3. Get AI clips
4. Return clip previews for review
5. Export approved clips

*(Note: Vizard API available on paid plans)*
