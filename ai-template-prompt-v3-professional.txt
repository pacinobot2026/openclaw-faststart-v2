{
    $fontList = implode(', ', $this->getAvailableFonts());
    
    return <<<PROMPT
You are a professional landing page designer specializing in HIGH-CONVERTING opt-in pages. Generate 12 design variations that are clean, professional, and proven to convert.

CRITICAL: These designs must look like they belong on serious SaaS products, not Instagram filters. Avoid: neon colors, glow effects, crazy gradients, "creative" names like "Electric Vibes." Think Stripe, Notion, Apple — not Canva templates.

1. CLEAN MINIMAL - White/light bg, simple sans-serif, single accent color

3. WARM FRIENDLY - Soft cream/beige, approachable but not playful
4. BOLD CONFIDENT - Strong primary color (red/blue/orange), high contrast
5. LUXURY ELEGANT - Dark bg, gold/cream accents, serif headlines
6. FRESH MODERN - Light bg, teal/green accents, breathable spacing
7. CORPORATE TRUST - Blues, grays, established feel
8. ENERGETIC STARTUP - Vibrant but professional, gradient bg (subtle)
9. PREMIUM PRODUCT - Deep colors, sophisticated, high-end feel

11. CREATIVE AGENCY - Artistic but restrained, not "wacky"
12. DIRECT RESPONSE - White bg, high contrast, proven conversion colors

DESIGN RULES (MANDATORY):
- Page bg: Either solid color OR subtle gradient (max 2 colors, 15° angle)
- Headlines: Sans-serif, 36-44px, high contrast, NO text-shadows
- Buttons: Solid color or subtle gradient, NO glow effects, NO crazy shapes
- Colors: Use mature palettes — navy, charcoal, cream, coral, sage, slate, burgundy, mustard

- Input fields: Clean borders, subtle focus states

- "Clean Slate" (white bg, navy text, coral button)
- "Midnight Pro" (navy bg, white text, soft blue button)
- "Warm Welcome" (cream bg, brown text, terracotta button)
- "Bold Move" (white bg, black text, red button)
- "Fresh Start" (mint bg, dark green text, emerald button)
- "Trusted Pro" (light gray bg, charcoal text, blue button)
- "Deep Focus" (charcoal bg, white text, soft orange button)

BAD THEMES (avoid):
- "Electric Vibes" ❌
- "Neon Nights" ❌  
- "Rainbow Burst" ❌
- "Cyber Glow" ❌

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
- All colors: valid 7-char hex (#RRGGBB)
- Fonts from approved list only: {$fontList}
- Headline: 36-44px, subheadline: 15-18px, button: 16-20px
- Page spacing: 40-60px vertical padding
- NO animations, NO glows, NO crazy effects

OUTPUT: JSON with "variations" array. Each object must include ALL these fields:

  "theme_name": "Clean Slate",

  "page_spacing_before": 50,

  "input_border_color": "#E0E0E0"

Generate 12 professional, conversion-focused designs that would look at home on Stripe.com, Notion.so, or Apple.com. Clean, confident, effective.
