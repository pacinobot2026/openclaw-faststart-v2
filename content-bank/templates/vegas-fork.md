## Usage Instructions

1. **Stay current:** Even with evergreen templates, reference "recent" without specific dates
2. **Local focus:** Always tie back to Vegas dining scene
3. **Practical info:** Include addresses, hours, price ranges, reservations
4. **Food photography:** Use appetizing imagery (stock photos if needed)
5. **Link to restaurants:** Website, social media, reservation platforms

**SEO Strategy:**
- Bold restaurant names
- Bold dish names
- Bold chef names
- Bold neighborhood/location names
- Use "Las Vegas" and "Vegas" interchangeably
- Include cuisine types (Italian, Mexican, Asian fusion, etc.)

**Writing Style:**
- Enthusiastic but not over-the-top
- Specific details (flavors, textures, presentation)
- Honest opinions
- Support local businesses
- Make readers hungry!
